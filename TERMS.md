## 5. Prohibited Content (Zero-Tolerance Policy)

You agree **NOT** to use the cloud sync service to store, transmit, or distribute any content that falls into the following categories. Violations may result in **immediate and permanent account termination** without prior notice.

### 5.1 Child Safety (Absolute Red Line)

- **Any sexual, sexualized, or suggestive content involving minors**, including but not limited to:
  - Sexually explicit depictions of minors (real or fictional, including AI-generated)
  - Sexualized imagery of minors (e.g., suggestive poses, swimwear/lingerie descriptions with sexual intent, fetishized depictions)
  - Grooming, enticement, or sexual solicitation content targeting minors
  - Romanticized or normalized depictions of adult-minor sexual relationships
- **Depictions of violence, abuse, torture, or cruelty directed at minors**, including physical, psychological, or emotional abuse portrayed in an approving, instructional, or gratifying manner
- **Content that endangers the physical or psychological well-being of minors** in any real-world applicable context

### 5.2 Terrorism, Violent Extremism & Organized Crime

- Content that **promotes, recruits for, incites, or glorifies terrorism** or designated terrorist organizations
- Content that **glorifies, romanticizes, or whitewashes** drug cartels, drug lords, organized crime leaders, or criminal organizations (e.g., heroic portrayals of narco-traffickers, mafia glorification)
- **Actionable instructions** for planning or carrying out terrorist attacks, mass violence, or acts of sabotage
- Content designed to **radicalize** individuals toward violent extremism

### 5.3 Drugs & Controlled Substances

- **Real, actionable instructions** for manufacturing, synthesizing, or refining illegal drugs or controlled substances
- Content that **promotes or normalizes drug use by minors**
- Content that **systematically glorifies drug trafficking** as a lifestyle or career aspiration

### 5.4 Weapons & Mass Harm

- **Real, actionable instructions** for manufacturing explosives, chemical weapons, biological agents, radiological devices, or other weapons of mass destruction
- **Detailed, usable instructions** for creating improvised weapons intended for mass harm
- Content designed to facilitate **real-world mass casualty events**

### 5.5 Hate Speech & Incitement

- Content that **incites violence, hatred, or discrimination** against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Content that promotes **genocide, ethnic cleansing, or systematic persecution**

### 5.6 Cults, Extremist Ideologies & Harmful Manipulation

- Content designed to **recruit for, promote, or provide operational guidance** for destructive cults or extremist sects
- Content that **instructs or encourages psychological manipulation, coercive control, or brainwashing techniques** targeting vulnerable individuals

### 5.7 Other Illegal & Harmful Content

- Content that **constitutes or facilitates real criminal activity**, including fraud, human trafficking, money laundering, identity theft, or cyberattacks
- **Non-consensual intimate imagery** (real or deepfake)
- Content that **systematically promotes self-harm or suicide** with actionable methods, especially when targeting vulnerable populations
- Content that **violates applicable laws** in the user's jurisdiction

### 5.8 Political Extremism

- Content that **directly incites violence** for political purposes, advocates for the **violent overthrow of governments**, or constitutes **political terrorism propaganda**

> **Important distinction regarding creative writing:**
> We fully respect the nature of fiction. Literary works naturally explore morally complex themes including conflict, violence, grey-area characters, power dynamics, and difficult social issues. **These Terms target real-world harmful, actionable, and illegal content — not fictional narratives exploring difficult subjects.**
>
> For example: A crime novel featuring a drug lord as an antagonist is legitimate fiction. A "how-to guide" romanticizing real drug manufacturing is not. A thriller involving a terrorist plot is valid storytelling. Recruitment propaganda for real terrorist organizations is not.
